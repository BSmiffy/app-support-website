A Simple Tool for Veterans

A simple, easy-to-use mobile app for veterans and their families. The VA Pay Date Calculator helps you quickly determine the estimated date of your first disability payment based on your effective date. No personal data is ever collected or stored.

Release Notes

Key Features

  • First Payment Date Calculation: Accurately calculates the date of the first regular monthly disability payment based on a user-provided "Effective Date," in accordance with VA payment rules (38 CFR ยง 3.31).
  • Clear Explanations: Provides simple, easy-to-understand explanations of how VA payments work, including the concept of paying "in arrears."
  • Direct VA Contact: Includes a clickable phone number that opens the device's native dialer, pre-filled with the official VA benefits help line number (1-800-827-1000).
  • Dual Theme Support: Features a fully functional Light Mode and Dark Mode to ensure user comfort in any lighting condition. The theme is easily toggled with a switch.
  • Cross-Platform Design: The user interface is designed to be clean and functional on both iOS and Android devices, with platform-specific adjustments for optimal user experience.
  • No Account Required: No need to create an account or provide an email to use the app.
  • Privacy Focused & Offline: The app is completely offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.
  • No Selling of Information: The app does not sell any information that is stored on your device or entered by the user.

Known Issues

  • UI on Niche Devices: Minor visual inconsistencies may exist on certain tablets or older phone models.
  • iOS Devices (All): The app name on the Home screen is missing spaces between each word. This will be fixed in a future update.
  • Android & iOS Devices: On devices with smaller screens, a user may need to scroll to see all information.
  • Android Fold Devices: Rotating the device will force the app to restart, requiring the user to re-enter the Effective Date.
  • Android Devices With Larger Screens: Text scaling is a bit small. Users with vision issues may have problems reading the text.

Limitations

  • No Payment Amount Calculation: This calculator does not determine the monetary amount of your payment.
  • No Back Pay Calculation: This version does not calculate retroactive (back pay) benefits.
  • Informational Use Only: The app is an estimation tool only and does not constitute legal or financial advice.
  • No Account or Data Storage: The app does not save any user-entered dates or have any account features.
