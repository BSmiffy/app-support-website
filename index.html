App Support & Privacy

A Simple Tool for Veterans

A simple, easy-to-use mobile app for veterans and their families. The VA Pay Date Calculator helps you quickly determine the estimated date of your first disability payment based on your effective date. No personal data is ever collected or stored.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our application.

Information Collection and Use

The VA Pay Date Calculator application does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information or any data from its users.

All calculations performed by the app are done locally on your device. The effective date you enter is used only for the calculation and is discarded when the app is closed. It is not saved, stored, or sent anywhere.
